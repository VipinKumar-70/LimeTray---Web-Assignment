About Us

At LimeTray, our journey began with a simple yet powerful insight — too many great restaurants were shutting down despite serving amazing food. Determined to make a difference, we set out to bridge the gap between restaurants and their customers through smart, tech-driven solutions.

We empower restaurants to go digital, connect with customers, and streamline their operations. In just three years, we've partnered with over 3500 restaurants, becoming one of the few global product companies from India. Backed by industry experts and Matrix Partners, our team of ISB and NSIT alumni is passionate about transforming how the restaurant industry thrives in the digital age.
